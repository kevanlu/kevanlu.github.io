                                          Neurophysiology of the Eye

                                          Kevan Lu Wednesday, March 18, 2015

                                          Lecture Overview

                                          • The next 2 lectures will focus exclusively on the neurophysiology of the visual system.
                                            • The first lecture will focus on phototransduction (the conversion of photon reception into action potentials).
                                            • The next lecture will focus on the CNS pathways that interpret visual information.

                                          Fundamental Components of the Visual System

                                          1. Eye
                                          2. Retina
                                          3. Optic nerves, optic chiasm, optic tract
                                          4. Lateral geniculate nucleus (LGN)
                                          5. Primary Visual Cortex (V1, or Striate Cortex)
                                          6. Extrastriate Visual Cortex
