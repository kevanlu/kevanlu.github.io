                                              Review of the Cerebrum

                                              • Components of the Cerebrum:
                                                1. Cerebral Cortex (Gray Matter)
                                                  • Superficial cortical layer of gray matter containing neuronal cell bodies
                                                2. Cerebral White Matter
                                                  • Deeper layer of white matter containing fibers connecting cell bodies to other cortical areas and the rest of the brain
                                                3. Deep Gray Matter
                                                  • Comprises the basal ganglia; the deepest layer of large collections of neuronal cell bodies.
