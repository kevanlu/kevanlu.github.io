    Schulich Notes is an online reference containing course notes written by medical students at the Schulich School of Medicine & Dentistry. We aim to make a resource that will facilitate studying, review, and exam preparation. Originally written and developed by Kevan Lu, the site has now expanded to include contributions from any Schulich medical student.
