    • Format is the same as the midterm.

    • Increased the amount of time from 2 hours to 2.5 hours.

    • 50 MCQ, 5 SA questions (for 50 marks as well).

    • Examined over all 5 weeks, emphasis on the latter 2 weeks.

    • Around 60% will be from the latter 2 weeks, 40% will be from the first 3 weeks.

    • No anatomy, embryology, etc.

    • Focus on major concepts, put special emphasis on:

      • Transplantation, ethics of transplantation.
