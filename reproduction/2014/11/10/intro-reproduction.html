                        Assignment

                        • Your goal is to try and write a clinical guideline for an Obstetrics and Gynecology journal.
                        • Due December 17, 2014.
                        • Writing a Clinical Guideline:
                          1. Review published studies relating to condition.
                          2. Determine strength of findings.
                          3. Grade evidence & make specific recommendations (Level A,B or C).
                        • Assignment Topics:
                          1. Primary Cesarean Section — is it better than 24 hrs of labour?
                            • In other words, compare an elective cesarean section vs 24 hours of labour.
                          2. Should Assisted Reproductive Technologies be offered to women over 50?
                          3. Should surgical intervention be offered to women at high risk for development of breast cancer?
                        • There may not be a ‘right or wrong’ answer, you just have to review the literature and make a decision on what you think is best.
                        • Assignment Format:
                          • 1000 words → font size 12, 4 double spaced pages, plus references.
                          • Include 4 sections:
                            1. Background.
                              • Full sentence narrative overview.
                              • Must be relevant, focused to clinical question.
                            2. Clinical Considerations & Recommendations.
                              • Point form, may use comparison tables.
                              • Identify/present relevant research.
                              • Discuss pros and cons of both sides, and then lean towards one.
                              • Must recognize strength of evidence, and draw conclusions based on this.
                            3. Summary of Recommendations & Conclusions (Level A, B or C evidence).
                              • List of recommendations and levels of evidence.
                              • Full sentence narrative on final recommendations.
                              • Answer the clinical question!
                              • Appropriately grade evidence, and support your conclusions.
                            4. References (max 15).
                          • You will be graded on style, spelling, and grammar.
                        • Have a look at ACOG Practice Guidelines
                          • They’re an excellent example of what is being looked for.
                          • If you follow this format, you should do well.
